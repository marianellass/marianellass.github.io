About me

I am an incoming Machine Learning Software Engineer at Permenance AI, where I will focus on developing real-world AI solutions. Before joining Permenance, I was a Venture Capital Summer Associate at Connecticut Innovations, evaluating and advising on AI-driven products in FinTech, ClimateTech, and Bioscience. I also spent two summers at RBC Capital Markets as a Software Engineer Intern, where I built AI chatbots and data visualization tools for quantitative traders.

I will receive my M.Eng. in Computer Science from Cornell Tech in May 2025. I earned my B.S. in Computer Science as an Honors and STEM Scholar, with minors in Math and Entrepreneurship, from the University of Connecticut in 2024. During my undergraduate years, I was involved in several research and leadership initiatives, including serving as Managing Director of Hillside Ventures, UConn's student-run venture capital fund, and as Principal Investigator on an IRB-approved study to improve equity and inclusion in STEM education.

My work bridges cutting-edge machine learning with practical business applications, from automating due diligence in venture capital to optimizing data flows in capital markets. At Permenance AI, I am excited to build on this experience by designing advanced AI solutions that drive meaningful impact across industries.
